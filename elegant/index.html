About ELEGANT Salon & Spa

Welcome to ELEGANT Salon & Spa, Houston's premier destination for luxury beauty treatments. Our mission is to provide exceptional service in a sophisticated, relaxing environment where you can unwind and rejuvenate.

Our team of licensed professionals is trained in the latest beauty techniques and uses only premium products to ensure you receive the highest quality treatments. We specialize in facials, hair treatments, keratin smoothing, waxing, threading, and spa services.

At ELEGANT Salon & Spa, we believe that beauty is an experience. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be treated to personalized care that leaves you looking and feeling your absolute best.
